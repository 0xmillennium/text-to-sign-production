# Candidate Cards

This surface stores candidate-level full evidence records. It is downstream of the family-level
[Candidate Universe](../candidate-universe/index.md) and is the main supporting surface for
concrete candidate analysis. Candidate-card instantiation does not mean scoring, vetoing,
selection, or final audit outcome.
