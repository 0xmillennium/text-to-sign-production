# Candidate Universe Record Template

This template is for family-level audit-universe records only. It is not a candidate-level record.
Family-level records must cite stable entries in the
[Source Corpus](../../source-corpus.md), using `SRC-*` IDs and heading anchors rather than
duplicating bibliographic entries.

## Family Type / Status

Allowed values:

- `primary_candidate_family`: A method family that may produce concrete candidate cards for later contribution selection.
- `counter_alternative_family`: A serious competing family included to avoid prematurely narrowing the audit universe.
- `boundary_family`: A family that defines technical, supervision, dataset, representation, or compatibility boundaries.
- `evaluation_family`: A family relevant primarily to evaluation design rather than model contribution selection.
- `frontier_watch_family`: A recent or heavy frontier family that may affect positioning or future work but may be out of immediate implementation scope.
- `background_context_family`: A family used for taxonomy or coverage context, not direct contribution selection.
